Interior/Exterior Windows

We started our business on windows. This is something we do better then anyone else. The process we use for your windows is:


  1. We use our microfiber brushes to lightly brush the outside windows
  2. Then we blow out any tracks in the outside windows with our nosel
  3. After the track is blown out we wipe it sparkling clean with a cloth
  4. We then use our solution and a window microfiber scrub pad and squeegee to make sure the window is completely cleaned
  5. Afterwards we will take any of the screens from your windows and use the microfiber scrub pad and our nosel to clean those as well.

Our window process is tried and true, we have been using it for X amount of years and constantly here from our customers that it leaves their windows cleaner then they have ever seen.

When it comes to gutters we will make sure that they are pristine. We have lots of experience with gutters and have a process that continues to prove that it is efficient and allows us to make sure your gutters are in tip top shape. Our process is:

Concrete

Concrete is something that if not done properly can cause many issues down the road. We will make sure it is proffesionally cleaned and no spots are left dirty. Our process of concrete cleaning is:


  1. We will assess the concrete by walking around and finding our starting point
  2. Then we use our heavy duty power washer and choose which of the nosel's are appropriate for the job
  3. We then power wash every inch of concrete perfectly to make sure there is no spot left unclean
  4. We then make sure to rinse it all of so there isnt a bunch of moss etc. on your freshly cleaned concrete

We will make sure the concrete will not be at risk of erosion and crumbling. We will also make sure to point out any issues that may arise if the concrete was not taken care of beforehand.
